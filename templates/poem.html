poem

Matter of time
Like leaves in Autumn,
Your falling reflects glory.
Like water of a mighty river,
Tear drops feels so holy. Every step I get closer,
Feels like I am being fooled by my guardian angel.
Every word seem true,
When seen from a different ane.
Some dreams only come true in the world of ants and fairies.
Its just a matter of time,
Your words roam my dairies
Sucidal thoughts, lets end this.
On your neck, theres a sharp knife Illusional clocks,
its a bad day, not a bad life
From autumn to summer,
you will forget our crimes.
Everything seems like peices of limes.
Its just a matter of time
Its just a matter of time


- Murtaza
